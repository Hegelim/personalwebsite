Me as research assistant at Goodly Labs, Inc.

In fall 2020, I participated in the Discovery Project at UC Berkeley Division of Data Science, and was the tech lead of the Public Editor validation team. We developed an algorithm based on Krippendorff's alpha, which is used to measure the level of agreement among users' input. We used our program to test the quality of Public Editor, and our program is listed on the CDSS's page.

My team project on Covid-19 Vaccine Distribution

In spring 2021, I partnered with another 3 UC Berkeley undergrad teammates in developing a project on Covid-19 vaccine distribution using decision theories, which include Decision Matrix, Minimax Regret, among others. We approximated economic impact for four age groups if they were infected by Covid, and deduced a vaccine distribution policy.
